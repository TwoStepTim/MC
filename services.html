County Services

Macon County offers a wide range of services to residents, businesses, and visitors. From permitting to tax payments, our services are designed to be simple, secure, and accessible — both online and in-person.

Below you’ll find our most commonly used services. Each listing includes a short summary and a direct link to begin your request or application.

Need Help Using Our Services?

If you have questions or need help accessing a county service, we’re here to assist you:

  • Email us at help@maconnc.org
  • Call us at (828) 555-1234, Mon–Fri, 8 AM to 5 PM
  • Or visit the Contact page for office locations and directions
